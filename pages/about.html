About
  • a margine ( ) is a publishing project that deals with the themes of interpretation and translation.
  • Every publication by is the result of an individual intervention and a further translation made by the designer itself.
  • Each author was assigned a book and a decalogue to help them during the first phase.
  • During the second phase, the firs interpretation passes through the filter of the designer that operates a new translation.
  • The publications are added to an online platform conceived as a mean to show books and to take part in the project.
